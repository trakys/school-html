Our Store

SerendipiTea is a quaint little two-story bubble tea shop in Toronto. This cafe is known for its modern and cozy design, making it perfect for photographers and teenagers who love Instagram. The second story holds multiple study booths, tables, and outlets, making it the perfect fit for people who need to study or even just for people who want some peace and quiet.

SerendipiTea mainly sells bubble tea, a drink that has recently become extremely popular for adolescents and adults, especially Asians. Bubble tea is a beverage made of tea and tapioca pearls. They come in countless different flavours, toppings, sizes and come see our menu by clicking the button at the top of this website. We also sell desserts, such as cheesecake and macarons, to go along with the drinks.

Our milk tea is one of our product lines. A product line is a series of different products that form a group, produced by the same company. The following are our product lines:

  • Milk tea
  • Fruit tea
  • Slushes
  • Desserts
